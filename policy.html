Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 5 July 2026 • Version 2.0

1. Introduction

A.R. Labs ("we", "us", "our") is dedicated to protecting your data and privacy. This Privacy Policy documents how AI-OS (the "Platform") accesses, collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you utilize our Services, register or sign in with your email credentials, or redeem access coupon codes.

2. Account Credentials & Data Collected

AI-OS uses local email and password credentials, or premium access coupon validation keys, as its secure authentication mechanism. When you register or sign in, we process:

  • Email Address: To establish your identity, associate your billing status (Basic or Premium), and manage account security.
  • User Profile Metadata: Full Name, Date of Birth, Gender, and Profession to customize operational timeline outputs and personalize your user dashboard.
  • Authentication Data: Passwords are cryptographically hashed using industry-standard bcrypt algorithms on the server before storage. One-Time Passwords (OTPs) generated during the verification process are stored temporarily and marked as used immediately upon validation to prevent reuse.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your collected information strictly to:

  • Maintain, personalize, and secure your account dashboard and custom settings.
  • Log your onboarding choices (Full Name, Date of Birth, Gender, and Profession) to customize roadmap timeline recommendations and business simulations.
  • Verify and log payment records (Razorpay IDs) to activate Premium subscriptions.
  • Identify you when you submit requests to our customer support or file inquiries.

4. Data Storage, Hosting & Security

Your account records and profile configurations are stored in secure PostgreSQL database tables. We apply industry-standard access tokens and TLS encryption to secure communication channels. Authentication sessions are stored locally in the browser via standard localStorage/sessionStorage blocks and managed via secure HttpOnly, SameSite cookies to keep you logged in across page reloads.

5. AI Prompt Processing

When you interact with the AI strategist or prompt tools, prompt instructions are transmitted securely to server-side APIs, which communicate with third-party generative models (including OpenRouter APIs). Please note that AI providers process prompts to generate responses according to their API services. We do not store your prompts permanently on our servers or log them to console outputs.

6. Payment Processing through Razorpay

All subscription upgrade transactions and billing details are processed securely and privately by Razorpay. A.R. Labs does not collect, access, or store raw credit/debit card numbers, CVVs, or cardholder credentials on our servers.

7. Media Processing & Cloudinary

We do not utilize Cloudinary or other external media hosting providers for user uploads. All profile layouts and components are styled locally.

8. Analytics & Tracking

To ensure maximum user privacy, the Platform does not integrate third-party analytical pixels, behavioral marketing tracking, or target advertising scripts.

9. Zero Data-Sharing with Third Parties

A.R. Labs maintains a strict zero data-sharing standard. We do not sell, rent, trade, or distribute your email address, name, or profile metadata to third-party advertising companies, analytical networks, or data brokers.

10. Data Retention

User profile and account information is retained for as long as the user account remains active. Operational logs and security audit trails are retained for up to 90 days to help detect and prevent abusive access or system errors.

11. Account Deletion, Opt-Out & User Rights

You have full ownership of your data. You may request account closure and data deletion at any time:

12. Cookie Usage

We use cookies and browser storage nodes solely to cache your dark/light theme preference, language settings, and current session parameters to enable continuous access. We do not use persistent tracking cookies for behavioral target marketing.

13. Policy Modifications

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. If we update our data management procedures, we will post the revised policy on this page with the updated "Effective Date" timeline.
