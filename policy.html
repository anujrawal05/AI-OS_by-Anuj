Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 5 July 2026 • Version 2.0

1. Introduction

A.R. Labs ("we", "us", "our") is dedicated to protecting your data and privacy. This Privacy Policy documents how AI-OS (the "Platform") accesses, collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you utilize our Services, register or sign in with your email credentials, or redeem access coupon codes.

2. Account Credentials & Data Collected

AI-OS uses local email and password credentials, or premium access coupon validation keys, as its secure authentication mechanism. When you register or sign in, we process:

  • Email Address: To establish your identity, associate your billing status (Basic or Premium), and manage account security.
  • User Profile Metadata: Full Name, Date of Birth, Gender, and Profession to customize operational timeline outputs and personalize your user dashboard.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your collected information strictly to:

  • Maintain, personalize, and secure your account dashboard and custom settings.
  • Log your onboarding choices (Full Name, Date of Birth, Gender, and Profession) to customize roadmap timeline recommendations and business simulations.
  • Verify and log payment records (Razorpay IDs) to activate Premium subscriptions.
  • Identify you when you submit requests to our customer support or file inquiries.

4. Data Storage, Hosting & Security

Your account records and profile configurations are stored in secure cloud database tables hosted by **Supabase**. We apply industry-standard access tokens and TLS encryption to secure communication channels. Authentication sessions are stored locally in the browser via standard localStorage/sessionStorage blocks to keep you logged in across page reloads.

5. Zero Data-Sharing with Third Parties

A.R. Labs maintains a strict zero data-sharing standard. We do not sell, rent, trade, or distribute your email address, name, or profile metadata to third-party advertising companies, analytical networks, or data brokers. All transaction logs and billing details are processed securely and privately by Razorpay, and we do not store raw credit/debit card numbers on our servers.

6. Account Deletion, Opt-Out & User Rights

You have full ownership of your data. You may request account closure and data deletion at any time:

How to Request Account & Data Deletion

If you wish to delete your profile, email, billing flags, and simulator history, please send an explicit deletion request from your registered email address to:

arproduction050@gmail.com

We will confirm your request and completely purge all account rows from our Supabase tables within five (5) business days.

7. Cookie Usage

We use cookies and browser storage nodes solely to cache your dark/light theme preference, language settings, and current session parameters to enable continuous access. We do not use persistent tracking cookies for behavioral target marketing.

8. Policy Modifications

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. If we update our data management procedures, we will post the revised policy on this page with the updated "Effective Date" timeline.

10. AI-OS Business Hub Data Management

When you interact with the AI-OS Business Hub tools (Launchpad, Strategist, Academy):

  • Simulated Inputs: The business models and budget ranges you select are processed client-side or sent to server-side APIs to compile custom outputs. We do not sell or monetize your business parameters.
  • Chat Logs & Custom Prompts: Conversations with the A.R. Business Strategist are stored locally in your browser session or processed via secure server connection layers.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal information, please write to:
