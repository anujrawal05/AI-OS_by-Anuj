Pro Tips

Use it right, get 10× results

✅
Do This
✓
Upload multiple PDFs to one notebook — cross-reference everything
✓
Name notebooks clearly: "Sem 4 Physics" not "Notebook 1"
✓
Ask specific questions: "What does Chapter 3 say about X?" not just "Tell me about X"
✓
Always verify source citations — click through to the original paragraph
✓
Listen to generated podcasts at 2× speed for fast revision
✓
Use it for YouTube videos you don't have time to watch fully
❌
Avoid This
✗
Uploading low-quality or scanned PDFs without OCR — it can't read images of text
✗
Asking vague questions like "summarize everything" — be specific
✗
Ignoring source citations — always verify before using in work
✗
Treating it like general ChatGPT — it only knows what you upload
✗
Skipping the podcast feature — it's the most underrated part
✗
Using it for real-time or internet-based research — it's offline from the web
