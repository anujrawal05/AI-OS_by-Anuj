Module 1

Business Fundamentals (The Engine)

▼
Concept Overview

Every business is simply a machine that exchanges value for money. To succeed, you must understand three numbers: Cost of Acquisition (CAC) (how much you pay to get a customer), Lifetime Value (LTV) (how much money they pay you over time), and Unit Economics (profit margin per sale).

Case Study: Unit Economics Analysis

Consider a custom merchandise store. Selling a t-shirt for ₹999. Manufacture costs ₹350, shipping costs ₹100, and marketing costs ₹250 to get one order. Margins = ₹999 - (350+100+250) = ₹299 profit. If your margin is too small or negative, your business will fail regardless of how much AI you use.

Practical Checklist
  • Calculate LTV and CAC before starting any business model.
  • Aim for a minimum LTV to CAC ratio of 3:1.
  • Design a recurring revenue model to reduce continuous acquisition costs.
Module 2

The Core AI Toolkit (Leverage)

▲
Using AI as Leverage

AI is not a business by itself; it is a force multiplier. Instead of hiring employees, use LLMs (like Llama-3, GPT-4) to automate content calendars, write marketing copy, handle support tickets, and build custom website elements instantly. This reduces overhead to virtually ₹0.

Practical Checklist
  • Build automated social media generation funnels.
  • Use voice-synthesis AI to produce advertising videos.
  • Deploy AI chatbots for instantaneous 24/7 lead capture.
Module 3

Modern Income Models (Monetizing)

▲
Sustainable Business Models

Four highly accessible models for beginners include:
1. AI Automation Agency (AAA): Build chatbots and automated funnels for local businesses.
2. Drop-Servicing: Sell digital designs/writing to clients, and generate them using high-end AI.
3. SaaS Micro-business: Build single-purpose utility web-apps using AI code engines and monetize via subscriptions.

Practical Checklist
  • Identify 3 target local businesses currently lacking website automation.
  • Assemble a portfolio of AI-generated digital designs in under 1 hour.
  • Set up a checkout page using Razorpay/Stripe to accept payments immediately.

    Practical Execution Blueprint (Launching)

    ▲
    Idea Validation with ₹0 Budget

    Never spend money building a product before people pay for it. Set up a simple landing page describing your offer. Pitch it to 50 prospects. If at least 3 offer to pay, your business is validated. Use AI to build the actual service after validation.

    Practical Checklist
    • Create a landing page using simple free builders in 30 minutes.
    • Draft cold outreach emails using AI and send to 50 potential clients.
    • Iterate or pivot instantly if zero purchase intent is verified.
